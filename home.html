About Me

Hi everybody! I’m David and I’m a native Portlander, drawer, sculptor, builder and painter. Throughout high school I used my skills to both design and create sets for my school’s theater department. I have had several years of wood shop and am experienced in the visual arts. I have applied those skills to theater by painting large backdrops, sets, and props.

Outside of school I have worked building decorative fences, Adirondack chairs, and doing backstage theater work (constructing, loading, and striking sets). Now that I am done with high school I am trying my hand at all sorts of media and am always excited to take on a new project. My longtime hobby is creating props and effects for my front yard Halloween graveyard display.

Please look through my work in the projects section and feel free to contact me if you are interested in working with me or commissioning a project.
